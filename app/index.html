Installation of solar panels by Sunway Solutions

Installation of solar panels by Sunway Solutions for customers includes the following steps:

  1. Initial Consultation: Our team conducts an initial consultation with the client to understand their energy needs and assess the possibilities of installing solar panels. We discuss the size of the system, the budget of the client, and provide the necessary information.
  2. Location analysis: We conduct a detailed analysis of the location where solar panels are planned to be installed. Explore the available space for installing panels to determine the most efficient configuration.
  3. System design: Based on the data obtained from the previous steps, our team develops an individual solar system design, taking into account the needs of the client.
  4. Providing an offer: We provide the client with a detailed offer, including the technical specification of the system, cost, installation time and warranty.
  5. Installation: After agreeing on the proposal and signing the contract, we proceed with the installation of solar panels. Our professional team carefully carries out the installation, including the installation of fasteners, solar panels and electrical connections.
  6. Grid connection: Once the solar panels are installed, we will connect your system to the electrical grid. This includes installing the inverter, connecting to the electrical panel, and making any necessary checks and adjustments.
  7. Testing and running: We test and validate the system to make sure it works and performs correctly. After successful testing, we provide you with a full report on the operation of the system and demonstrate its functionality.
  8. Training and service: We provide customer training on how to use and manage the solar system. In addition, we offer after-sales service to regularly check and maintain the system to ensure reliable operation throughout its life.

Demontage of solar panels

Solar panel demontaging is the process of removing and disassembly solar modules, which can be carried out in the following cases: when replacing old panels with new ones, moving or moving the system, renovating a building, or when maintenance and repair if required. Below are the steps that are usually included in the process of demontaging solar panels:

  • Assessment and preparation: At the beginning of the process, an assessment and planning of demontaging is carried out.
  • Demontaging of photovoltaic modules: Each solar module must be carefully removed from its mountings.
  • Removing the Mounts: After removing the modules, proceed to remove the mountings that hold the solar panels in place.
  • Disconnecting electrical connections: An important step is to disconnect the electrical connections and wires associated with the solar panels.
  • Packing and transport: After demontaging, the components of the solar system must be packed and prepared for transport.
  • Disposal and recycling: After demontaging the solar panels, the waste must be properly disposed of and recycled.

INSTALLATION OF A CHARGING STATION FOR CARS

The installation of solar panels in combination with car charging offers several advantages and optimal solutions for electric mobility infrastructure. Here are a few reasons why this combination is beneficial:

  • Renewable and environmentally friendly energy: Solar panels generate electricity from solar radiation, making it completely renewable and environmentally friendly. Charging your car with solar energy helps reduce your dependence on fossil fuels and reduce your carbon footprint.
  • Economic benefit: Installing solar panels allows you to produce your own electricity for free. This reduces or eliminates the cost of buying electricity from the grid to charge the car. As a result, EV owners save on monthly electricity bills.
  • Independence from the grid: Installing solar panels combined with car charging ensures independence from the grid. You can charge your car directly from the solar panels, even if the grid is turned off or if there is a lack of power.
  • Optimal use of solar energy: Charging your car using solar energy allows you to optimally use the generated energy. The times when solar energy is most intensely generated often coincide with peak car charging periods, especially during the day.
  • Selling excess energy to the grid: In case your car is fully charged and the solar panels are still generating energy, you can sell excess electricity back to the grid. This allows you to receive income or compensation for the energy you produce.

As a result, the installation of solar panels in combination with car charging provides an environmentally friendly, cost-effective and independent solution for charging electric vehicles. It makes optimal use of solar energy, reduces energy costs and contributes to a more sustainable and environmentally responsible transport system.
